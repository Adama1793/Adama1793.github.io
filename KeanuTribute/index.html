A Tribute Page to Keanu Reeves

...More than just Ted Logan

A Brief Biography

Keanu Reeves was born in September 2, 1964 in Beirut, Lebanon. His mother was Patricia Taylor, a former showgirl-turned-costume designer and his father, Samuel Nowlin Reeves, was a geologist. The marriage fell through however, leading his mother to remarry and divorce three times soon after. Keanu would move around a bit before settling in Toronto, Canada. There he grew a love of passion for theater and hockey. Keanu is dyslexic and had a hard time in high school, this led him to drop out. He faced a decisive choice, a career in hockey or acting? He was talented in both. Picking the Former, his first film was a supporting role in a sports film about hockey, Youngblood(1986). Keanu was also a motorcyclist enthusiast which left him in a near-death accident in 1988 on Topanga Canyon Blvd leaving him a vertical scar on his stomach.

A year later he landed his first popularizing role, Bill and Ted’s Excellent Adventure(1989), as Ted Logan alongside actor Alex winter. Although popularizing, Keanu feared to be only known for his work as Ted Logan. This Lead him to take on more serious projects such as My Own Private Idaho(1991) with his best friend River Phoenix, who would soon pass away two years later from drug overdose.

Keanu saw box-office growth with Speed(1994) and The Matrix(1999) cementing his stardom. In 1999 Keanu and his wife, Jennifer Syme, gave birth to their stillborn daughter Ava Archer Syme-Reeves. They divorced a few weeks after and then in 2001 she passed way in a car accident. Unfortunately, a year and a half later his sister Kim Reeves, who was battling leukemia, would pass away.

After a brief grief period, Keanu finished the matrix trilogy. He didn’t stop there, Keanu went on to star in blockbuster hits like Constantine(2005), Scanner Darkly(2006) and Day the Earth Stood Still(2008). Trouble found Keanu again in 2008 when He faced legal issues with paparazzi Alison Silva claiming Keanu allegedly hit and injured him with a Porsche, after a harrassing year and a half later Keanu won the case.

In 2012, Keanu has even produced and starred in a documentary about celluloid and digital film making Side by Side. He also made his directorial debut with Man of Tai Chi(2013). His most recent success is John Wick(2014) , John Wick 2(2017) and there’s a third on the way dated to release in 2019. Even today Keanu Reeves is known for his selfless and kind nature. He’s a humble and grateful man with a loving outlook on life. Even with tragedy hit time after time he still lives life with such positivity.

Keanu’s Many Achievements and Contributions:

  • He was also a musician in such bands as Becky and Dogstar.
  • He’s even performed theater at the Manitoba Theatre Centre as hamlet.
  • He wrote the text to a picture book Ode to happiness.
  • He supported charities such as SickKids Foundation and Stand Up To Cancer
  • He also set up his own personal cancer charity choosing not to attach his name to it.
  • He cofounded a production company that later produced the movie Henry’s Crime which he stars in
  • He cofounded a motorcycle company named Arch Motorcycle Company, that builds and sels custom motorcycles.
